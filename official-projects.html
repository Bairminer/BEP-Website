Water Sensor

By BEP

This is a project on how to build a water sensor.

You will need:

  • 2 × 1 kΩ resistors
  • 1 × 10 kΩ resistor
  • 2 × NPN transistors
  • A few wires
  • 1 × LED
  • 1 × 9 V battery
